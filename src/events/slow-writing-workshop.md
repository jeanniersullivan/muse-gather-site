Bring a notebook or a laptop and a cup of something warm. We'll write together for ninety unhurried minutes — a few gentle prompts, long stretches of quiet, and a little time at the end to share a sentence if you'd like to.

No experience required. This is not a critique group; it's a room that makes it easier to begin. Everyone leaves with something started.

A recording is available afterward for anyone who can't make it live.
